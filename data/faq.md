# Microsoft AI Tour - Frequently Asked Questions

## Registration & Check-in

**Q: What time should I arrive?**
A: Doors open at 7:30 AM. We recommend arriving early to avoid lines at registration and grab a good seat for the keynote.

**Q: Do I need to print my ticket?**

A: No, show your QR code on your phone at the registration desk. You'll receive a badge to wear throughout the event.

**Q: Can I register on-site?**

A: Walk-in registration is available but subject to capacity. Pre-registration is strongly recommended.

## Food & Drinks

**Q: Is lunch provided?**
A: Yes, complimentary lunch and refreshments are provided for all registered attendees in the networking area.

**Q: Are there vegetarian/vegan options?**
A: Yes, vegetarian, vegan, halal, and gluten-free options are available. Please check food labels or ask catering staff.

**Q: Can I bring my own food?**
A: You can bring your own food to eat in designated areas, but outside food is not permitted in session rooms.

**Q: Is there coffee available?**
A: Yes, coffee stations are available throughout the venue during registration, breaks, and lunch.

## Sessions & Content

**Q: Can I attend any session I want?**
A: Yes, all sessions are open to all attendees on a first-come, first-served basis. Popular sessions may fill up, so arrive early.

**Q: Will sessions be recorded?**
Not sure yet

**Q: Can I ask questions during sessions?**
A: Yes! Most sessions have Q&A time. 

**Q: What should I bring?**
A: Bring your phone (for QR code), laptop (for workshops), charger, and business cards for networking.

## Workshops

**Q: Do workshops require pre-registration?**
A: Some workshops have limited capacity and require separate registration. Check your confirmation email for details.

**Q: What do I need for hands-on workshops?**
A: Bring a laptop with a modern browser. Most workshops use GitHub Codespaces, so no local setup is needed. An Azure subscription (free tier works) may be required.

**Q: I couldn't get into a full workshop. What should I do?**
A: Check back 5 minutes before start time for no-shows. Workshop materials are usually available on GitHub afterward.

## WiFi & Connectivity

**Q: Is there free WiFi?**
A: Yes, free WiFi is available throughout the venue. Network: "AI Tour Guest" - no password required.

**Q: The WiFi is slow. What should I do?**
A: The venue has high-capacity WiFi, but with many attendees it can be congested. For workshops, consider using mobile data as backup.

## Networking & Social

**Q: What's the event hashtag?**
A: Use #MSAITour on social media to share your experience and connect with other attendees.

**Q: Is there a networking area?**
A: Yes, the Connection Hub is the main networking area. Microsoft partners and experts will be available throughout the day.

**Q: How can I connect with speakers?**
A: Many speakers are available in the networking area after their sessions. Check the event app for speaker meet-and-greet times.

## After the Event

**Q: How do I access session materials after the event?**
A: Presentation slides and code samples are typically shared on GitHub. Check the session pages for links.

**Q: Will I receive a certificate of attendance?**
A: Certificates are available for workshop completions. General attendance certificates can be requested at the registration desk.

**Q: How do I provide feedback?**
A: A feedback survey will be sent to your registered email after the event. Your feedback helps us improve future events!
